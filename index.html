About GreenHarvest

Cultivating Health & Wealth

GreenHarvest Thailand is a fully licensed medical cannabis cultivation company based in Thailand.

We grow pharmaceutical-grade, medical cannabis in controlled-environment facilities and sell directly to government-approved buyers, hospitals, and research institutions. Our model allows investors from around the world to participate in a Plant-Backed Revenue Partnership Program.

Mission

Creating a transparent, profitable investment ecosystem while supplying clean medical cannabis.

Vision

To become Southeast Asia’s most trusted and scalable medical cannabis cultivation partner.

Understanding Risks

All agricultural investments carry risk, including crop failure, disease, pest infestation, and regulatory changes.

While we use controlled environment agriculture and global best practices to reduce risk, returns are not legally guaranteed. Investors should review all documents carefully.
